Description

This lasagna recipe takes a while to make, but it is one of the best you will find online! Source

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 gloves crushed garlic
  • 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 6.5 oz can tomato sauce
  • 6 oz can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil leaves
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp italian seasoning
  • 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
  • 0.25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 12 oz sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Pay the cheese tax.
  2. Gather all your ingredients.
  3. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheeese with egg, remaining parsley, and 0.5 tsp salt.
  7. Preheat over to 375F (190C)
  8. To assemble, spread 1.5 cupes meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with half of the ricotta mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  9. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan. Cover with foil ensuring the foil is not touching the cheese.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  11. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
